# Privacy Policy

## Danmaku Loader — Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** 2025-05-31

### Data Collection

Danmaku Loader does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

- All operations are performed **locally** within your browser.
- The extension only accesses the active tab to interact with the video player.
- No data is sent to external servers.

### Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **`activeTab`**: To access the current video page for弹幕 injection.
- **`scripting`**: To inject the弹幕 renderer into the video player.

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of loading local弹幕 files into the video player.

### Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
